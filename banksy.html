Different controversial art pieces by Banksy around the globe.

Ghetto 4 Life

Painted in 2013 on a grey wall at 153 Street and Elton Avenue

Deemed controversial due to possibly reinforcing a negative stereotype about the borough of Bronx

Free Zehra Dogan

Painted in 2018 in the corner of Houston Street and the Bowery

Protests the imprisonment of the Turkish-Kurdish artist and journalist who was imprisoned for painting the destruction of a predominant Kurdish town

One Original Thought

Painted in 2014 on the corner of Jay Street and Water Street

Quote by the Greek Cynic philosopher Diogenes of Sinope
