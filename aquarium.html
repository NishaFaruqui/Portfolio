The Secret Life of Jellyfish

Float into the mystical water world of Jellyfish Bloom at the NY Aquarium. This special exhibit features over 13 different species of these translucent creatures, such as the Moon Jellyfish, Upside Down Jellyfish, and Pacific Sea Nettles. Dive deeper and discover more about how we raise these jellies, and other marine life. You will also have the opportunity to create your own digital jellyfish and even touch the Moon jellies (don't worry...they are harmless!) Plan your next visit for the ultimate jellyfish experience.
